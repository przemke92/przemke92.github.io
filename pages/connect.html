Career Journey

My professional journey began at Franklin Templeton Investments in January 2016, where I served as a

Collateral Analyst. In this role, I was responsible for monitoring equity, fixed income, and foreign exchange trades, as well as derivatives' settlement. I adeptly resolved issues with both internal business units and external clients, ensuring margin requirements were met promptly and collateral movements were accurately settled. I also investigated and resolved collateral balance discrepancies, responded to settlement queries, and prepared daily, weekly, and monthly reports. My participation in global meetings and close collaboration with trading desks allowed me to contribute to high-quality service delivery and process improvements.

In April 2017, I transitioned to the role of Data Operations Analyst at Franklin Templeton Investments. This position expanded my focus to the critical area of data integration. I monitored the FTS dashboard, ensuring data feeds met SLAs and resolved data processing exceptions. I became proficient in troubleshooting and resolving errors from system interfaces to investment management systems, researching and analyzing ESB data and data services. I also played a key role in maintaining data integrity, analyzing new data requests, and creating ad hoc and scheduled reports. This role honed my analytical skills and ability to work with complex data systems.

Expanding my skills beyond finance and data analysis, I took on a part-time role as an English Tutor at Tutlo from March 2020 to December 2022. This experience allowed me to develop my communication and teaching abilities in a web-based online environment. It also showcased my adaptability and commitment to continuous learning.

In January 2023, I joined Bunge as a Data Integration Analyst/Business Systems Analyst, becoming a member of the Integration Competency Center. Here, I am responsible for EAI between multiple systems within the Bunge worldwide group. My duties include process and data analysis, working in both development and maintenance streams (3rd line support). I work in a Scrum environment, focusing on data mapping and validation using Jira, integrating data between various systems, and conducting data analysis using Oracle SQL. I collaborate closely with developers, providing them with necessary reports and mapped data, and am involved in numerous projects integrating data from areas like Risk Management, Accounting, Logistics, Exchange Trading, and more. This role has further deepened my expertise in data integration and business systems analysis, allowing me to contribute to significant projects on a global scale.

It's not just gathering requirements, it's engineering business success through strategic IT alignment.
